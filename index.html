Looking at the plot above, there is a slightly increasing trend to the top ten hottest days every year since 2006. However, the R-square value of this trendline is very low, indicating that there is a weak relationship between the trendline and the data. A longer time period, more locations and more statistical analysis would be necessary to determine if there is a statistically significant upwards trend.
